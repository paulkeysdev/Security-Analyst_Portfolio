Hello!I am a dedicated Security Analyst specializing in identifying vulnerabilities before they become threats. With a background in threat intelligence and incident response, I help organizations navigate the complex landscape of modern cybersecurity. I don't just find holes in a perimeter; I build sustainable frameworks that protect both data integrity and user trust. My approach combines rigorous technical auditing with a clear understanding of business risk, ensuring that security is an enabler—not a bottleneck.

                   

With a background in computer science and a keen interest in continuous learning, I strive to stay updated with the latest industry trends and best practices. I'm dedicated to writing clean, maintainable code and delivering high-quality solutions.
